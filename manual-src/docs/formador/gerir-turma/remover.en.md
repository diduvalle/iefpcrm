# Removing a trainee

Take away a trainee's access to the class.

## Step by step

1. **Settings → Class trainees** — locate the trainee.
2. Click **✕** on their row.
3. Confirm.

The **access stops working** immediately.

!!! warning "Local data is not affected"
    Removing access **does not delete** the work data that is on the **trainee's computer** (the *sandbox* is local). Only the **online access** to the class is removed.

!!! note "You can't remove yourself"
    For safety, you can't remove your own administrator account.
