# Adding an individual trainee

To create access one at a time, without the import file.

## Step by step

1. **Settings → Class trainees → Add individually**.
2. Fill in:
    - **Name** *(required)* and **Surname**.
    - The trainee's **Email**.
    - **Username** — if you leave it empty, it is **generated automatically** from the name (e.g.: `joao.silva`).
    - **Password** *(required)*.
    - **Role** — Trainee (default) or Trainer.
3. **Add to class**.

The trainee appears in the list, ready to log in with the link `?t=…`.

!!! tip "Co-trainer"
    To have a **co-trainer**, choose the *Trainer* role — they will have management access to the class.
