*Preview and save the proposal as PDF.*

Generate the **A4** document, ready to print or save.

## Step by step

1. Open the proposal → action bar.
2. **Preview** — see the "sheet" as it will come out.
3. **Download PDF** → in the browser's print window, choose **Save as PDF**.

The document uses the proposal's **[template](../modelos.md)** (header, logo, quotation, totals, terms, signature) and the **client's data**. The footer stays fixed at the bottom of each page.

!!! tip "Switch template"
    Each proposal can use a different **template**. Try switching and exporting again to compare.
