# Loss reason & reopen

When a proposal does not move forward, you record **why** — for later analysis.

## Recording the loss

When you change the status to **Lost** (in the modal or by dragging to the *Lost* column), the app asks for:

- **Reason** — Price / Competition / Timing / No budget / No response / Needs changed / Features / Other.
- Free **note** (optional).

!!! tip "What it's for"
    The reasons feed the **[Loss analysis](../analytics.md)** in Analytics — you see the biggest cause of loss in your base.

## Reopen

Changed your mind? On a *Lost* (or *Won*) proposal, the **Reopen** button puts it back into **Negotiation**.
