# Working in the CRM

Your workspace has several **modules**, in the sidebar. Here's what each one does — for the full detail, see **[Features](../modulos/index.md)**.

## Main

- **Dashboard** — overview: KPIs, revenue, pipeline, shortcuts.
- **Companies** — entities with tax data (NIPC, CAE, address).
- **Contacts** — the people (customers), linked to companies.
- **Products** — catalogue (family → subfamily → item).
- **Proposals** — pipeline in **Kanban**; export each proposal as a **PDF A4**.
- **Calendar** — calendar and tasks.

## Marketing & Analysis

- **Campaigns** — marketing by segment (with a per-persona generator).
- **Email** — compose and track emails (opens/clicks).
- **Analytics** — funnel, conversion, CAC, LTV, *lead scoring*.

## Compliance

- **GDPR** — consents, data subject requests, RoPA, breaches. See **[GDPR & Pedagogy](../rgpd/index.md)**.

## Tips

!!! tip "Experiment without fear"
    You're in **your** *sandbox*. Create, edit and delete freely — nothing affects your colleagues. To restore the sample data, there's a button in **Settings → Data & backup**.

!!! tip "Learning tooltips"
    Notice the **ⓘ** icons next to various titles — they explain the concept and point to the relevant UFCD slides.
