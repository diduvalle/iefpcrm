# Submit my work

When you finish the exercise, you hand everything in from within the CRM itself.

## Before submitting: the signature

The **signature is mandatory** (it certifies that the work is yours). If you don't have one yet:

1. Click on **your name** (bottom-left corner) → **My profile**.
2. Under **Email signature**, write/format your signature.
3. **Save changes**.

## Submit

1. In the sidebar, click **"Submit your work"**.
2. Confirm the **trainer's email** (it comes pre-filled) and, if you like, write a **message**.
3. Click **Submit work**.

Your work (a *snapshot* of the CRM: customers, proposals, etc.) is delivered to the trainer, and they receive a notification.

!!! warning "You have 2 submissions"
    You can submit a **maximum of 2 times**. The counter shows **1/2** and then **2/2**; on the third, it becomes locked. Submit when you're truly ready.

!!! info "What the trainer sees"
    The trainer can **review** your work (customers, proposals) and **generate the PDF** of your proposals — using the template you designed.
