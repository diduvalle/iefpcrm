# Data breaches

Record of **security incidents** that affect personal data (loss, unauthorised access, leak…).

## Each breach

- **Date** and **Description** of the incident.
- **Affected data** and **number of data subjects**.
- **Severity** and **measures** taken.
- **Notified to the CNPD?** and **to the data subjects?**

## Step by step

1. **GDPR → Breaches tab**.
2. **+ Breach** → describe the incident and the affected data.
3. Record the **measures** and the **notification** status.

!!! warning "The 72-hour deadline"
    A risk breach must be **notified to the CNPD within 72 hours**. If there is a **high risk**, the **data subjects** must also be informed.
