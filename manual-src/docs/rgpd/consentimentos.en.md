# Consents

Record of each data subject's consent to the processing of their data.

## Each consent

- **Customer/Data subject** it relates to.
- **Purpose** — marketing, newsletter, relationship management…
- **Legal basis** — Consent, Contract, Legitimate interest, Legal obligation…
- **Collection channel** and **Date**.
- **Status** — Active / Withdrawn.

!!! info "Valid consent"
    It must be **freely given, specific, informed and unambiguous**. The data subject can **withdraw it** at any time.

## Step by step

1. **GDPR → Consents tab**.
2. **+ Consent** → choose data subject, purpose, legal basis and channel.
3. **Save**.
4. To **withdraw**, change the status to *Withdrawn* (or the data subject does so on the **[Portal](portal.md)**).
